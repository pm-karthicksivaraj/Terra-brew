TerraBrew feature roadmap — six categories of additional features to differentiate from competitors

🔒 Compliance & Regulatory Depth

Compliance

Multi-regulation engine unique

  • UK Forest Risk Commodities Act support
  • Japan Green Transformation (GX) readiness
  • Korea deforestation bill (in development)
  • Single dashboard, multiple regulation profiles
  • Auto-flag when new regulation applies to a shipment
Compliance

Risk scoring engine unique

  • Per-plot deforestation risk score (0–100)
  • Based on proximity to forest edge, slope, protected zones
  • Red/amber/green flags on every DDS
  • Auditor-ready risk narrative auto-generated
Compliance

DDS version control

  • Full history of every DDS revision
  • Diff view between versions
  • Rollback and re-submission workflow
  • EU customs amendment tracking

🤖 AI & Intelligence Layer

AI

Satellite deforestation detection unique

  • Automated change detection vs. 2020 baseline
  • Monthly polygon re-verification via Sentinel-2
  • Alert when plot boundary overlaps new clearing
  • Evidence image captured + timestamped to DDS
AI

AI compliance copilot unique

  • Chat interface: "Is this shipment EUDR-ready?"
  • Auto-fill DDS fields from uploaded documents
  • Translate field agent voice notes to structured data
  • Flag missing evidence before submission
AI

Yield & climate intelligence

  • Weather overlays per farm plot
  • Harvest prediction from NDVI satellite data
  • Drought / flood risk scoring per season
  • Climate risk included in investor ESG reports

🌍 Market & Supply Chain Expansion

Market

Buyer-side portal unique

  • EU importers log in to verify supplier DDS
  • One-click DDS acceptance/rejection workflow
  • Buyer compliance dashboard across all suppliers
  • Reverse onboarding: buyer invites their suppliers
Market

Multi-commodity expansion

  • EUDR also covers cocoa, soy, palm oil, cattle, rubber, wood
  • Reuse same compliance engine for new crops
  • Cocoa-specific entity models (Ghana, Côte d'Ivoire)
  • Shared farmer registry across commodities
Market

Cooperative self-service app

  • Offline-first mobile app for field agents
  • GPS plot capture without internet
  • Sync on next connection
  • SMS-based farmer registration fallback

🔐 Trust & Verification Infrastructure

Trust

Trust Score™ unique

  • Composite score per exporter (0–1000)
  • Based on DDS history, audit flags, data completeness
  • Visible to EU buyers as supplier signal
  • Becomes a moat — exporters protect their score
Trust

Third-party audit integration

  • Rainforest Alliance / UTZ audit data import
  • 4C Association verification linkage
  • ISO 14001 environmental cert storage
  • Auditor role with read-only portal access
Trust

Public QR traceability page

  • Consumer-facing "scan the bag" landing page
  • Farm origin map, harvest date, EUDR status
  • White-label for roasters and retailers
  • Premium upsell for brand-conscious buyers
