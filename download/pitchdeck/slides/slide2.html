The Problem: A Regulatory Earthquake

EUDR Bans Deforestation-Linked Products

Requires GPS polygon data, chain-of-custody records, and legally binding Due Diligence Statements for every shipment.

Non-Compliance = Market Lockout

Shipments without valid DDS turned back at EU borders. No appeals. A binary regulatory gate.

Vietnam Is Disproportionately Exposed

$3.5B in annual coffee exports to EU. Most cooperatives rely on spreadsheets. The gap is existential.
